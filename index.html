Events

24th April

Haldi 4:30PM - 5:30PM

Haldi ceremony is where the bride and groom are covered in turmeric paste. A paste is prepared with turmeric (Haldi), sandalwood, and curd, this is then rubbed upon the bride and groom before a ceremonial bath. A joyful ceremony where friends and relatives are in a playful mood and have fun applying the haldi paste on the bride and groom.

Sangeet 7PM - 12AM

During this ceremony families from both sides meet each other and sing, dance along. This will have a celebratory festival feel to it with people wearing vibrant clothes, dancing, singing, playing games and a lot more.

25th April

Barat 7PM-8:00PM

A celebratory wedding procession for the groom involving live music and dancing will start in the evening. As guests arrive, they are divided into the bride and groom's side. The bride's side will be directed to a meeting place while the groom's guests join him on his processional entry.

Wedding 11:30PM-1AM

This is the main event in the entire wedding when the bride and the groom tie the knot. The couple circles sacred fire and are finally declared husband and wife.
