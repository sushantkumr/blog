Events

24th April

Haldi 4:30PM - 5:30PM

The Haldi ceremony is where the bride and groom are covered in turmeric paste. A paste prepared with turmeric (Haldi), sandalwood, and curd, and then this is rubbed upon the bride and groom before a ceremonial bath. A joyful ceremony where friends and relatives are in a playful mood and have fun applying the haldi paste on the bride and groom.

Sangeet 7PM - 12AM

During this ceremony families from both sides meet each other and sing, dance along. This will be having a celebratory festival feel to it with people wearing vibrant clothes and dancing, singing, playing games and a lot more.

25th April

Barat 7:30PM-8:00PM

A celebratory wedding procession for the groom involving live music and dancing will start in the evening. As guests arrive, they are divided into the bride and groom's side. The bride's side will be directed to a meeting place while the groom's guests join him on his processional entry.

Wedding 11:30PM-1AM

This is the main event in the entire wedding when the bride and the groom tie the knot. As maximum number of guests would be attending this event. This is when the couple circle the sacred fire and are finally declared husband and wife.
